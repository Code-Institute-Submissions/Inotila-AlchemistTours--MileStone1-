Namibian Attractions

Namibia has many attractions to offer! With a vast landscape, diverser wildlife, multiple cultures, and amazing sunsets.

The must see places are:

Etosha

Type: Wildlife Reserve


Location: Northern Namibia


Main Attractions: Lions, Rhinons
and Cheetahs

Windhoek

Type: Capital City


Location: Northern Namibia


Main Attractions: Joes Beer House,
Independence Museum, and Kapana

Swakopmund

Type: Costal City


Location: Western Namibia


Main Attractions: Desert, Ocean
and Architecture
