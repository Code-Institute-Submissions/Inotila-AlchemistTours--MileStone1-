About Namibia

Typical weather

Namibia is mostly high plateau Along the West coast is the Namib Desert To the East is the Kalahari Desert The northern border is a flat area,with the Kunene river drawing the boarder line between Namibia and Angola. This part has been designated by the the World Wildlife Fund as part of the Angolan Mompane Woodland Eco-region. While the Orange River divides the Namibian and South African boarder.
